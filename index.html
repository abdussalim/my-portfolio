From physics curiosity to connected products

A cleaner way to tell the story

I build from curiosity, then refine until the interface feels obvious.

My path started with physics and Arduino, then grew into web applications, Internet of Things projects, and practical dashboards. The common thread is simple: make technical things feel lighter for the people who need them.

Scrollytelling

Physics trained the way I notice systems.

Before web apps, I learned to think in cause, measurement, and feedback. That habit still shapes how I debug and design.

02

Arduino and sensors gave me a direct bridge between code and the physical world, where small details matter quickly.

03

Web apps turned prototypes into tools.

04

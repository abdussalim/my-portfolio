About Me

Hi guys, let me introduce myself. My full name is Abdus Salim but you can call me Sarimu. Since I was in high school I've fallen in love with STEM (Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics), especially in Physics. When I was in college, I got interested in programming. In the last year of my study, my friends introduced me to websites and asked me to help them build Website Applications (Web-Apps).

I hadn't learned about making a website before — I only understood how to program microcontrollers, especially Arduino. But they believed in me, so I tried my best and successfully helped them. I'm very grateful because I learned a new skill in Websites and the Internet of Things (IoT). Here I'm, a fresh graduate from Lambung Mangkurat University.
